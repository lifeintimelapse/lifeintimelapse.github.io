3d Printed Window Latch Spacers

16th Feb 2015 3dprinting

The balcony PVC doors and windows in our flat are a bit past their prime, but they'll do for a few years yet. They've proved almost impossible to get parts for though, a case in point being the spacers between the latches. These have become rather unattractively discoloured and some have simply broken and fallen off. Another problem easily solved with 3d printing.
